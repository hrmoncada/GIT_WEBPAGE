 		Bio

                     




Dr. Moncada is a parallel computing specialist. He earned his Ph.D. in Computational Science from UTEP-US in May 2018. He also holds a BS in Physics from UNMSM-Peru and MS in Applied Mathematics from UNM-US. He worked for two years (Jan 2019 to Jan 2021) as a postdoctoral research associate at Los Alamos National Laboratory on the E3SM ECP project. He is currently working as a research scientist at the Computer Science in the  University of Texas at El Paso on the GAMESS ECP project.

His research interests are in software development for scientific computing applications, parallel computing, performance modeling and analysis, and performance optimization. He is also a fellow of the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM) Parallel Processing for Scientific Computing, and the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) Transactions on Parallel Computing

Dr. Moncada enjoys practicing outdoor and indoor activities. He practiced kickboxing and jiu-jitsu at the UFG gym in El Paso. The practices of these self-defense sports are for fun and keeping a healthy body. He also enjoys hiking with his two mixed bread dogs Babka and Honeybear.
